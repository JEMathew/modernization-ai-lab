# FAQ

## Is this an AI wrapper?

No. The platform vision is built around Enterprise Context Graph, long-running workflows, specialized agents, human review and reusable modernization knowledge.

## What is available today?

The current MVP demonstrates AI-assisted database modernization artifacts for an Oracle → BigQuery use case.

## Why not use Gemini directly?

Gemini is a model layer. Enterprises need workflows, context, approvals, validation, artifact management and governance around model capabilities.

## Why human-in-the-loop?

Modernization decisions affect production systems, business reporting and enterprise risk. Critical decisions must remain reviewable and auditable.

## What is the long-term vision?

An Enterprise Modernization Operating System for cloud and AI-ready data platform modernization.
